What the data is saying

Six read-outs that shaped the recommendations on the Action Center page.

Revenue

Revenue is broken down across months, categories, and individual products to separate steady performers from one-off spikes.

Category

Health & Beauty stands out as one of the strongest revenue categories in the catalogue.

Retention

Repeat customers make up a small share of the base — a clear retention opportunity rather than an acquisition problem.

Delivery

Delivery performance surfaces real operational bottlenecks, tracked here by state and by order status.

Products

Top and bottom product analysis flags both the growth areas and the ones quietly dragging on performance.

Documentation

Every DAX measure behind the report is documented separately, so the modeling is auditable, not a black box.

Executive summary

The case-study close — why this report exists and what it's for.

This project turns Olist's raw e-commerce data into a working sales analysis dashboard — covering revenue, product performance, customer behavior, and delivery risk in one connected report.

Beyond the visuals, every measure is documented on its own appendix pages, so the modeling choices are visible and reviewable rather than hidden inside the file.
